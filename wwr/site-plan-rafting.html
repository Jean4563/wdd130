Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to provide details and dates of where you can do the rafting activity in Peru, give the necessary guide of equipment and things you need to know before riding a boat.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Any adventurer who wants to start in the world of rafting.

What do they want? Every adventurous beginner can enjoy rafting without fear.

What are their needs that aren't being met? Information on equipment needed for this activity, places where it is practiced and how to start if I have never practiced it before.
